Who We Are

We, at Hello Santa, are a group of young professionals with a common vision of spreading joy and happiness all around. With the goal of bringing a smile to everyone’s face, we are here to deliver a bundle of surprises and excitement wrapped in shimmery hopes.
Story Behind Our Name

Our name is related to what we do. We want to build a brand which is easy to remember and directly highlight our work.
OUR VISION


We have a common vision of building an easy place to look when someone thinks of gifting something to his/her loved ones.
In today’s world, when friendship is taken by social media goals and relationships take the form of long distance. When we are becoming social on social media but not to our surroundings, Giving something to the person who is important to us keeps the feeling alive.


But the problem is what to gift?
Our vision is to take down this problem and provide the best suited gifting solution.


In addition to this, we wanted to make the gift unique that can only resemble someone special. To fulfill this, we started building a community which will solve this problem. From here we started building an artist community.
Art is not something which can be drawn on paper only. Every eye pleasing thing can be considered as art and with this idea we are bound to deliver unique and hassle free gifting experience.
