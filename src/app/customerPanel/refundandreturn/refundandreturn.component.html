Dear browsers, vendors, customers, and merchants,

We expect your co-operation and support in completing the process, and that will allow us to resolve your complaint expediently.

  • In case of the recipient's improper handling of the product, no replacement or refund will be provided.

  • Wrong address given by the customer: The product will be delivered to the correct address after confirming the address from the customer, and a standard shipping charge will be levied on the customer.
  • Mistake from the company side in address: The product will be delivered to the correct address without extra charges.
  • Unsuccessful Delivery: Unsuccessful deliveries arising from the recipient not being present at the time of delivery at the address provided by the sender or the recipient refusing to accept the delivery. In such a case, free delivery will be attempted to the recipient’s address twice. If still the delivery is not completed, then the customer/sender will be contacted for 3rd attempt, and this 3rd attempt will be charged with standard delivery charges and will only be attempted on confirmation from the customer/sender to the address reconfirmed by the customer/sender.
  • Cancelation of order: Cancellation of order is allowed if such request is made within 5 hours after ordering the product, and Hello Santa will make a full refund in such cases.

SECTION 3 – REFUNDS

The refund is initiated only after we get back the product. We can refund 100% of the amount as a special discount coupon which can be used on your next order or 90% of the amount to the original payment method.

To be eligible for a return or refund, your item must be unused and in the same condition that you received it. It must also be in the original packaging. Once we receive the returned product, we will notify you of the approval or rejection of your refund. If you are approved, then your refund will be processed.

Hello Santa will process online refund on confirmation over mail or phone call to the customer or recipient. Once processed by Hello Santa, the refund might take 10 to 15 business days to reflect in your account. There are certain situations where only partial refunds are granted: (if applicable)

  • Any item not in its original condition is damaged or missing parts for reasons not due to our error.

Late or missing refunds (if applicable):

  • If you haven’t received a refund yet, check your bank account again.
  • Then contact your credit card company; it may take some time before your refund is officially posted.
  • Next, contact your bank. There is often some processing time before a refund is posted.
  • If you’ve done all of this and you still have not received your refund yet, please contact us at support@hellosanta.in.

Only regular-priced items may be refunded. Sale items cannot be refunded or may be refunded only after deducting a certain amount consisting of convenience, payment gateway and internet handling charges.

SECTION 4 – RETURNS

Customized products are exempt from being returned.

To complete your return, we require a receipt or proof of purchase. Once your return is received and inspected, we will email you that we have received your returned item.

Exchanges (if applicable):

  • We only replace items if they are defective or damaged. If you need to exchange it for the same thing, send us an email at support@hellosanta.in.

If there is no one available at the shipping addresses to accept your order at the time of delivery, the order will not be considered late.
